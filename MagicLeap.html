Magic Leap Gateway

Bridging the gap between familiar and future.

Magic Leap is an American start up company that is working on technology for augmented reality that projects a digital light field into the user's eye using silicon photonics. They have yet to release any products but there is some speculation - leaks - regarding the form factor of the glasses. The patents mention a separate hardware component, the gateway, but there is no indication as to what that might look like. This project is a 3 week exercise exploring what the gateway could look like. It might take some time for people to be completely comfortable with total gestural control so I set out to ease the transition of augmented reality into the home entertainment space while maintaining the utilization of its technology. The main component of the design is the remote control. Without the glasses, the remote is a dead-front, smooth, uninterrupted surface. It's not until the user puts on the glasses that they can see the controls which are projected onto the surface.
