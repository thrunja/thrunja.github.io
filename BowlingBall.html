Bowling Ball Package

Industrial Design Studio 1
In order to support a regulation 16lb bowling ball, a package needs to be strong. Using a honey comb shape
for strength, the package is folded together using just one sheet of E-Flute corrugated board. My experiences
as a grocery store stock boy influenced my decision to focus the design on the ability to stack as well as
maximizing the pallet to limit the amount of air that is shipped without sacrificing aesthetic.
