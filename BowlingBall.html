Bowling Ball Package

Industrial Design Studio 1
For this studio project we had to design a bowling ball box that supports a 16lb. bowling ball.
Using my experiences as a grocery store stock boy, I decided to focus my design on the ability to stack as well as maximizing the pallet
to limit the amount of air that is shipped without sacrificing aesthetic. Using a honey comb shape for strength,
the package is folded together using just one sheet of E-Flute corrugated board.
