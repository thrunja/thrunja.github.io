FISKARS FLORAL PRUNERS

Ergonomic study focusing on comfort and efficiency.

Dan Formosa, co-founder of Smart Design, said, "If we understand what the extremes are, the middle will take care of itself." Using this idea, I began with focusing the design around elders who frequently garden. Specifically my grandmother who can't grip her floral pruners anymore. Using a big to small gear ratio, the blade can open full width while the handle only needs to open half the distance of normal floral pruners. This also allows the pruners to be used quickly, which is ideal for professional florists.
