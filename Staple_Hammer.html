Arrow Fastener
Staple Hammer Redesign

Industrial Design Studio 2

Arrow Fastener's staple hammer is an iconic product. However, it is time for a change or two...or three. I set out to solve the issues of comfort, safety, and aesthetics. With the use of color and by keeping the affordance of Arrow's signature chrome head, I was able to match the brand language of Arrow Fastener.
