BEDMATE

Lofted bedshelf for college dorm rooms.

Bedmate is a customizable lofted-bed shelf for college dorm rooms. The shelf is designed to be injection molded with powder coated steel brackets that clamp the shelf to the head of any size bed. With inspiration from Dieter Rams' design philosophies, good design can be introduced into the college dorm room.
