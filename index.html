Hello!

My name is Jack. I am a second year industrial design student at the University of Cincinnati.
For as long as I can remember, I wanted to be a professional hockey player, a fire fighter, and an astronaut. I wanted to do it all.
My favorite toys being Lincoln Logs and Lego, I figured maybe I wanted to be an architect.
When I heard of famous designers Raymond Loewy and Dieter Rams, I knew exactly what I wanted to be. I aspire to be the next great designer.
With my passion to create and my desire to help people, industrial design is the perfect match for me.
