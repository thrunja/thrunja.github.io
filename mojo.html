VIRB MOJO

Action camera for the active millennial.

Virb Mojo is the newest (conceptual) member of the Garmin action camera family. Active millennials aren't doing the extreme sports that action cameras were originally created for but they still like to explore, create experiences, and ultimately share those experiences online with their friends. Mojo is waterproof up to 10 meters and shoots 4k ultra HD / 30 fps making it the perfect adventure companion to take places you wouldn't dare take your phone. Being able to share directly to your favorite social media platforms is one of the many benefits of Mojo over other action cameras.

*Completed during summer 2017 internship at Garmin.
