Opulent Peril

Industrial Design Studio 1
Opulent Peril is a chess set inspired by a luxurious yet edgy and bad-ass aesthetic very similar to steam punk.
Focusing on family of form, the measurements of each piece, as well as the relationship between the pieces
as a whole, are derived from The Fibonacci Percentages. To enhance the visual hierarchy, the detailing on
the top of each piece is directly inspired by the moves that that piece is allowed to make.
